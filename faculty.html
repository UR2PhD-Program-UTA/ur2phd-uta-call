Call for Faculty Proposals – UR2PhD at UTA (Fall 2025 Only)

We are now accepting proposals from UTA faculty to lead a UR2PhD research cohort during Fall 2025.

What is a Cohort?

  • 1 faculty mentor
  • 1 graduate student mentor
  • 2 undergraduate researchers

Cohorts conduct research and participate in a national virtual course that supports research skills and graduate school preparation.

Proposal Requirements

  • Project description
  • Recommended undergrad skills and eligibility
  • Identified graduate mentor
  • (Optional) 2 undergraduate researchers

Deadline: August 19, 2025

If undergraduate researchers are not yet identified, we will issue a call to CSE undergrads and allow faculty to select from the applicant pool.

Graduate Mentor Details

  • Course options: Tuesdays 2–3PM CT or Wednesdays 11AM–12PM CT
  • Course runs September 9 – December 10, 2025
  • $1,000 stipend paid by CRA
  • Max 2 unexcused absences; completion required
  • International students: confirm stipend eligibility with UTA OIE

Additional Benefits

  • Travel funding up to $2,000 for presenting research
  • Up to $8,000 REU continuation funding for undergrads
  • $250/year for continued graduate mentorship

Questions? Contact Dr. Cesar Torres at cearto@uta.edu
