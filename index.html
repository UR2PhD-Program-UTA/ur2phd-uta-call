UR2PhD at UTA – Call for Faculty Proposals (Fall 2025 Only)

We are now accepting proposals from UTA faculty to lead a UR2PhD research cohort during Fall 2025.

What is a Cohort?

A cohort consists of:

  • 1 faculty mentor
  • 1 graduate student mentor
  • 2 undergraduate researchers
Cohorts conduct research and participate in a national virtual course that supports research skills and graduate school preparation.

Faculty Proposal Requirements

  • Short project description
  • Recommended undergrad skills and eligibility
  • Identified graduate mentor
  • (Optional) 2 undergraduate researchers

Deadline: August 19, 2025

If undergraduates are not identified, we will issue a call to CSE undergrads, gather applications, and allow you to choose students for your project.
